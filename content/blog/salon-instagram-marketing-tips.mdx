## Why Salons Need Instagram

**Hair salons** are visual businesses. **Instagram** is ideal for:

- Showing before/after work and styles
- Announcing new services, products, or stylists
- Building trust with a polished, consistent feed
- Letting clients book or DM you easily

**Salon Instagram marketing** works best when you post regularly and mix promotions with inspiration.

## Salon Instagram Best Practices

- **Post 3–5 times per week** — Consistency matters more than perfection  
- **Use your brand** — Same colors, logo, and style in posters and captions  
- **Show your work** — Before/after (with permission), new styles, trends  
- **Add location and hashtags** — So local clients can find you  
- **Use Stories** — For last-minute slots, promos, and behind-the-scenes

## FAQ

### How do I get more clients from Instagram for my salon?

Post before/after work, promos, and booking info regularly. Use location and relevant hashtags, respond to DMs, and keep a consistent, professional look.

### What should a hair salon post on Instagram?

Mix before/after photos, new styles, offers, team intros, tips, and booking reminders. Use posters for promos and events so your feed looks cohesive.

### How often should a salon post on Instagram?

Aim for 3–5 posts per week. Use Stories for daily updates like last-minute availability or quick promos.
