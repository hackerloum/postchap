**WhatsApp Status** is one of the most powerful ways to market your business in Africa. Unlike feed posts, **WhatsApp Status** is seen by your contacts every day — and it’s free. This guide shows **how to use WhatsApp Status to market your business** with updates, promos, and simple posters.

## Why WhatsApp Status Works for African Businesses

**WhatsApp Status business marketing** works because:

- **Reach** — Your contacts see your status when they open WhatsApp  
- **Frequency** — You can update daily (stories disappear after 24 hours)  
- **Trust** — People expect updates from contacts; it feels personal  
- **Low cost** — No ad spend; just your time and content  

**WhatsApp Status** is especially strong in Nigeria, Kenya, Tanzania, Ghana, and across Africa where WhatsApp is the main messaging app.

## How to Use Status for Business

1. **Use a business account** (optional but useful) — WhatsApp Business lets you add profile details and quick replies.  
2. **Post regularly** — At least one update per day so you stay visible.  
3. **Mix content** — Promos, behind-the-scenes, tips, and “we’re open” reminders.  
4. **Use visuals** — A clear **poster** or graphic gets more attention than text alone.  
5. **Add a CTA** — “DM to order”, “Call now”, “Visit today” so people know what to do.  

**WhatsApp Status business marketing** is most effective when your status is useful or interesting, not only salesy.

## What to Post on WhatsApp Status

- **Daily special** — Today’s offer or menu item  
- **Opening hours** — “We’re open until 8”  
- **New product or service** — Short visual + one line  
- **Event or promo** — Countdown or reminder  
- **Thank you** — After a busy day or week  
- **Tip or quote** — Builds value and keeps you top of mind  
- **Behind-the-scenes** — Prep, team, or process  

Keep **WhatsApp Status** updates short and scannable. A **poster** with one message (e.g. “20% off today”) works better than long text.

## Conclusion

**Using WhatsApp Status to market your business** in Africa is free and effective. Post daily, use clear visuals and posters, and add a simple call to action. **WhatsApp Status business marketing** helps you stay visible to your contacts without a big budget.

## FAQ

### How often should I post on WhatsApp Status for my business?

At least once per day. Status disappears after 24 hours, so daily updates keep you visible. You can post 2–3 times if you have different messages (e.g. morning offer, evening reminder).

### What size should a WhatsApp Status poster be?

Use vertical format: 1080×1920 px works well (same as Stories). This fills the screen and looks sharp.

### Can WhatsApp Status really bring customers?

Yes. Contacts see your status when they open WhatsApp. If your updates are useful (promos, hours, new products), they’ll remember you and reach out when they need you.

### Do I need a designer for WhatsApp Status?

No. Use a poster maker or AI tool to create simple graphics. One clear message per status works best.
