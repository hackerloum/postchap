## What Is an AI Poster Generator?

An **AI poster generator** is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to create poster designs. You provide:

- Your **brand** (logo, colors, industry)
- **Context** (occasion, message, platform)

The AI then generates layout, text placement, and visuals so you get a **professional poster** without opening a design app. Many tools also let you regenerate or tweak until you’re happy.

## How AI Creates Posters

**AI poster generation** typically works in steps:

1. **Input** — Your brand kit, occasion (e.g. Eid, sale), and optional copy  
2. **Layout** — The AI picks a structure (headline, image area, CTA)  
3. **Visuals** — It selects or generates images and graphics that fit your brand  
4. **Text** — It places and styles headlines and body text  
5. **Output** — You get a ready-to-use image (e.g. for Instagram or print)

The result is a **poster** that looks consistent with your brand and is optimized for the format you need.

## FAQ

### Is an AI poster generator really free?

Many tools, including ArtMaster, offer a free plan with a set number of generations per month. Check each tool’s pricing for limits and upgrades.

### Do I need design experience to use an AI poster generator?

No. You provide brand details and context; the AI handles layout and design. You can still edit text or regenerate if needed.

### Can AI posters be used for print?

Yes. Export at high resolution (e.g. 300 DPI) when your tool supports it. For social media, standard export sizes (e.g. 1080×1080) are usually built in.

### How is an AI poster generator different from Canva?

Canva is template-based: you choose and edit. An AI poster generator creates a full design from your brand and occasion, so you spend less time designing and more time running your business.
